FAQ

Common Questions

Who can advertise on Ontario's Best?
+
Any legitimate Ontario-based business or operator serving Ontario customers. For iGaming categories (casinos, sports betting, poker), you must hold a valid AGCO licence and be registered with iGaming Ontario. We verify this before any placement goes live. For tourism categories (hotels, attractions), there's no regulatory requirement — just a quality bar. We don't accept adult content, unregulated gambling operators, or businesses with unresolved consumer complaints.
How long does it take to go live?
+
Typically 3–5 business days from payment confirmation. We need your logo, description copy (unless you're on Premier, where we write it), and your destination URL. iGaming placements take a couple of extra days for compliance review. We'll send you a preview link before publishing so you can approve the placement before it goes live.
Can I cancel?
+
Yes. All packages are month-to-month with 30 days' written notice required to cancel. If you cancel mid-month, your placement stays live for the remainder of the paid period. We don't offer partial refunds on monthly fees, but we don't lock you into long contracts either. Some advertisers choose to prepay quarterly or annually for a discounted rate — ask us about this when you enquire.
Can I advertise bonuses or promotional offers?
+
No — and this applies across all iGaming placements. Ontario's AGCO regulations prohibit advertising bonuses, free spins, promotional credits, or any inducement in public-facing affiliate content. This is not our rule — it's a legal requirement. Your listing focuses on platform quality, game selection, trust signals, and user experience. If you want to communicate offers to existing registered players, that's handled through your own platform's CRM, not through us.
Is there a revenue-share option instead of flat monthly?
+
Yes, for iGaming operators. We can structure CPA (cost-per-acquisition) or revenue-share agreements where we earn a commission on conversions we drive rather than charging a flat monthly rate. This works well for operators with strong conversion rates and clear tracking infrastructure. We discuss this as part of the Custom iGaming Partnership package. Contact us to explore what structure makes sense for your situation.
How many sponsored slots are available per category?
+
We cap sponsored placements per page to maintain editorial integrity and ensure each advertiser gets meaningful exposure. Generally 2–4 slots per category page, depending on the page. The slot count shown on this page is updated in real time as spaces fill. If your preferred category is full, we'll add you to a waitlist and contact you when a slot opens — typically within 1–3 months as contracts roll over.
