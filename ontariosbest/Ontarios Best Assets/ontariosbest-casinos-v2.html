Frequently Asked Questions

Is online gambling legal in Ontario?+
Yes. Ontario launched its regulated online gambling market on April 4, 2022 — the first province in Canada to do so. Private operators can legally offer online casino games, sports betting, and poker to Ontario residents, provided they are licensed by the AGCO and registered with iGaming Ontario. Playing at an AGCO-licensed site is fully legal for Ontario residents aged 19 and over.
What is the legal gambling age in Ontario?+
The legal online gambling age in Ontario is 19. All AGCO-licensed casinos are required to verify your age and identity through KYC checks before you can make a withdrawal. This is a hard regulatory requirement, not operator policy.
Are casino winnings taxable in Ontario?+
Generally, no. Gambling winnings are not taxable for casual players in Canada. The CRA does not consider gambling income taxable unless you are a professional gambler — someone for whom gambling is a primary and structured income source. For the vast majority of players, winnings from licensed Ontario online casinos are tax-free. Consult a tax professional if gambling represents a significant portion of your income.
How do I verify a casino is legally licensed in Ontario?+
Look for the iGaming Ontario and AGCO logos in the site's footer. You can also cross-reference the official AGCO registry and iGaming Ontario's operator directory to confirm active status. Every casino listed on ontariosbest.com has been verified against these registries. We recommend confirming status directly with regulators before depositing at any site not on our list.
Can I use Interac at Ontario online casinos?+
Yes. Interac is the most widely supported payment method at AGCO-licensed casinos and the fastest option for both deposits and withdrawals. Horseshoe Casino currently offers the fastest processing in our testing, with funds often arriving within hours. Note that licensed casinos use a closed-loop system — you can generally only withdraw via a method you've previously deposited with. Cryptocurrency is not permitted at any AGCO-licensed casino.
What responsible gambling tools are available?+
All AGCO-licensed casinos are required by law to offer deposit limits, loss limits, wagering limits, session time reminders, and self-exclusion options. Ontario also operates the My PlayBreak program, allowing players to self-exclude from all licensed Ontario gambling sites simultaneously. These tools are accessible from your account settings on any licensed platform.
