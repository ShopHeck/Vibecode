Ontario's Poker Market

What Makes Ontario's Poker Scene Unique

Ontario is the only province in Canada with a regulated online poker market. Since the AGCO launched on April 4, 2022, six major rooms operate legally — with one critical constraint: ring-fenced liquidity. You play only against other players physically located in Ontario.

Smaller pools than international sites, but full regulatory protection: segregated funds, certified RNG software, mandatory responsible gambling tools, and formal AGCO dispute resolution.

⚖️

Shared Liquidity: A Ruling to Watch in 2026

In November 2025, a Superior Court of Ontario ruled that international shared liquidity is legal under current law — potentially opening Ontario's player pool to cross-border markets. That ruling is under appeal with a decision expected mid-2026. If upheld, it could transform Ontario's online poker landscape entirely. We'll update this page when a ruling is issued.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is online poker legal in Ontario?+
Yes. Online poker has been legal and regulated in Ontario since April 4, 2022. Only AGCO-licensed rooms registered with iGaming Ontario may legally offer real-money poker. Six rooms currently hold active licences. Playing at unlicensed offshore sites is technically in a grey area — iGaming Ontario advises players to stick to regulated rooms.
Why can I only play against Ontario players?+
Ontario's AGCO licence requires a ring-fenced model — you can only play against other players physically in Ontario, enforced through mandatory geolocation. A November 2025 Superior Court ruling found international shared liquidity may be permissible, but that decision is under appeal with a mid-2026 decision expected. If upheld, Ontario could become the first North American jurisdiction to permit formal cross-border poker liquidity.
Are poker winnings taxable in Ontario?+
For recreational players, no. The CRA does not treat gambling winnings as taxable income for casual players. Taxation only applies when poker is your primary profession — structured, deliberate, and your main income source. Consult a tax professional if you're a high-stakes or full-time player.
Which room has the most traffic?+
GGPoker Ontario leads with approximately 57% of Ontario's cash game market and 350–400 active seats during peak hours (8–11 PM ET). PokerStars Ontario is second. BetMGM Poker, PartyPoker Ontario, and Bwin share a player pool on the PartyPoker network, making their combined liquidity competitive.
What is the best room for beginners?+
888poker Ontario consistently offers the softest competition in the province — the top choice for recreational and beginning players. GGPoker Ontario has the most traffic and best learning tools through PokerCraft. PokerStars Ontario has the strongest freeroll and low-stakes tournament programme for players building a bankroll from scratch.
Can I play on global PokerStars or GGPoker from Ontario?+
No. Ontario residents must use the Ontario-specific versions — PokerStars Ontario and GGPoker Ontario — which operate on separate ring-fenced pools. The global ".com" versions will detect your Ontario location and redirect or block you. Each Ontario room has its own distinct software client, account system, and player pool entirely separate from international play.
