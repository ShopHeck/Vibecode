## How We Review Casinos

Every casino review on this site follows the same process:

1. **Registration & Verification** — We create an account and go through the full KYC process
2. **Deposit & Bonus Claim** — We test the minimum deposit, payment methods, and bonus activation
3. **Game Testing** — We play slots, table games, and live dealer games across devices
4. **Support Testing** — We contact customer support via chat and email to test response times and quality
5. **Withdrawal Testing** — We initiate a withdrawal and track the time from request to receipt
6. **Licence Verification** — We confirm the operator is listed on igamingontario.ca before publishing

We score casinos across five categories: Games & Software, Bonuses & Promotions, User Experience, Customer Support, and Payments. The overall rating is a weighted average of all five.
