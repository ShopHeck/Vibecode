**Date:** 2026-04-01
**Track:** 2 — Authority & Monetization

## Overview

OntariosBest.com is positioned as Ontario's premier lifestyle authority — covering online casinos, travel, dining, entertainment, events, and services. The content strategy builds organic authority first, then converts traffic to revenue through affiliate CPA, sponsored placements, and display advertising.

**Approach:** Pillar-cluster architecture by vertical + city hub pages for local intent + a trend/events content layer for recurring traffic spikes.

**Cadence:** 3–5 high-quality, AI-assisted pieces per week. Trend content is additive (published within 48h of a newsworthy event), not replacing the weekly schedule.

## Content Architecture

### Three Tiers

**Tier 1 — Vertical Pillars (6)**
Comprehensive evergreen hub pages. Authority anchors that cluster content links back to.

| Pillar | URL | Primary Intent |
|--------|-----|----------------|
| Online Casinos | `/casinos/` | Transactional — affiliate CPA |
| Travel & Tourism | `/travel/` | Informational → affiliate |
| Dining | `/dining/` | Informational → sponsored |
| Entertainment | `/entertainment/` | Informational → sponsored |
| Events | `/events/` | Trending → repeat visits |
| Services | `/services/` | Informational → sponsored |

**Tier 2 — Cluster Content**
Long-tail posts answering specific queries, funnelling into the relevant pillar. 3–5 per pillar per month. Target: 800–1,200 words each.

**Tier 3 — City Hubs**
Geographic landing pages aggregating the best across all verticals for one Ontario city. These are the inventory sold to local businesses for sponsored placements.

| Hub | URL | Launch |
|-----|-----|--------|
| Toronto | `/ontario/toronto/` | Month 2 |
| Ottawa | `/ontario/ottawa/` | Month 2 |
| Niagara Falls | `/ontario/niagara-falls/` | Month 2 |
| Hamilton | `/ontario/hamilton/` | Month 3+ |

### Internal Linking Model
- Every cluster post links up to its vertical pillar AND sideways to the relevant city hub
- City hubs link down to individual reviews and listings
- Pillar pages link to their top 5 performing cluster posts
- No orphan content — every post has at least 2 internal links on publish

### On-Page SEO Requirements (every piece)
Applied via Rank Math. These are the content brief defaults:

1. **Focus keyword in:** title (first 60 chars), opening paragraph, one H2, URL slug, meta description
2. **Schema:**
   - Blog/cluster posts → `Article`
   - Casino reviews → `Review` + `AggregateRating`
   - City hubs + listings → `LocalBusiness`
   - FAQ sections → `FAQPage`
3. **Word count targets:**
   - Casino reviews: 1,500–2,500w
   - Cluster posts: 800–1,200w
   - City hubs: 2,000w+
   - Trend/news pieces: 600–800w
4. **Internal links:** minimum 2 per post on publish (pillar + city hub)
5. **Update cadence:**
   - Casino reviews: every 6 months (bonus changes)
   - City hubs: quarterly
   - Monthly event posts: updated in place, not republished

### E-E-A-T Requirements (casino vertical — YMYL)
Google applies heightened scrutiny to gambling content. Every casino piece must include:
- Author byline with bio linking to author page
- "Last reviewed" date displayed on review
- iGO license verification callout / trust badge
- Responsible gambling link in footer (already implemented in theme)
